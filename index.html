No internet? Use WhatsApp or SMS

Text "pharmacy open now" or your area name to get a list of open pharmacies — no app navigation needed.

How it works

  1. Save +1 (246) 535-BOT in your contacts as Pharmacy Bot
  2. Send any of these messages on WhatsApp or SMS:
    • pharmacy open now
    • pharmacies in Bridgetown
    • pharmacy St James
    • late night pharmacy
  3. Receive a plain-text list of open pharmacies with phone numbers within seconds

Powered by the same real-time data as this page. Works on any phone — no data plan required for SMS.
