Search for pharmacies

See which pharmacies are open now, anywhere in Barbados. Filter by parish or type, or use your location to find one near you.

Some pharmacies give out free or subsidised medication through the Barbados Drug Service — free at government polyclinics, or for a dispensing fee at participating private pharmacies. Others, marked “Private (no benefit)”, are not in the scheme. Tick “Free & subsidised medicine” to show only those that take part. Opening hours can change, so call ahead to confirm.

Where this information comes from

Government and Barbados Drug Service pharmacies come from the Barbados Drug Service register, verified May 2026. Pharmacies marked “Private (no benefit)” are drawn from a wider public pharmacy list and have not been confirmed with the Barbados Drug Service — they may still offer subsidised medicine, so call to check.

Opening hours and Drug Service participation can change — call ahead to confirm.

If a pharmacy is refusing your Barbados Drug Service (SBS) prescription, contact the Barbados Drug Service before you pay.
